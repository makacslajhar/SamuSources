The boy and the tree.
There is a boy,
and there is a tree.
The boy love the tree.
The tree is huge and healthy.
It has lot of leaf.
It has huge shadow.
The boy like the shadow.
It is cool,
and very comfortable.
The boy like to sit under it.
He has a watercan.
He use it, to water the tree.
The tree love the boy.
The boy love him
and help him.
He trend the tree leafs.
The tree leafs is green.
The boy grow up.
The tree is older too.
The boy does not help the tree.
The tree is upset.
But the boy has a son.
The son see the tree.
He love the tree, like his father.
He start to trend it.
The tree is happy again.
It is the end.
The bird and the apple.
There is bird.
It is hungry.
It see an apple.
The apple is red.
It is on a tree.
The bird go for the apple.
It is in front of the apple.
It want to eat it.
But the apple has feelings.
It say this: Don't eat me.
I'm not tasty.
The bird started to think.
Little later it is said this:
I am hungry and you are tasty.
The apple say this: There is another apple, eat that not me.
The bird is thinking.
After that it is say this: Okay. I won't eat you.
It is go to the other apple.
It is green.
The green apple do not speak.
The bird is happy.
It eat the green apple.
It is full now.
It is go to the red apple.
And say this: Thank you for the meal.
The red apple yas: You are welcome.
It is the end.
