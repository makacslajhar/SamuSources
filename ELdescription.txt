The 3 tales are made from "Basic English" words
(https://simple.wikipedia.org/wiki/Wikipedia:Basic_English_alphabetical_wordlist),
but the third has 14 more words in it.
These are:
-want
-catch
-tell
-freedom
-listen
-frighten
-understand
-realize
-belong
-nature
-human
-around
-eat
-life

They're suitable for 5-10 years old children.
You can find 12 triplets in tale1.
You can find 8 triplets in tale2.
You can find 5 triplets in tale3.
